The Brief

A dental clinic that needed
to earn trust online.

Yakut Dental Clinic is a modern dental practice led by Dr. Omar Yakut in Novi Pazar, Serbia. They needed a bilingual website that would build trust, showcase their expertise, and convert visitors into patients.

We built a fully custom multi-page website featuring bilingual support (SR/EN), before & after galleries, an online booking system, detailed service pages, and a dental implants section — all delivered within 48 hours.
